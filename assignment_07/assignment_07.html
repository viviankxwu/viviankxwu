Topic

This website will be a digital portfolio for my achievements and a consulting opportunities page, detailing my experiences beyond a regular resume. I'd like to use this website to share projects I've worked on, campaigns I've led, initiatives I've spearheaded, and ultimately people I've touched.

Audience

My intended audience include recruiters for internships and jobs as well as brands and organizations I can partner with. This website showcases tangible, visual representations of my impact and expertise.

Design & Style

Since this is a website-version of a resume, similar to a portfolio, I'd like to keep the design and color scheme professional and aesthetic. I will lean towards using black, white, and grays, and additional pops of color will be pastel colors.

Color Palette

  • Primary colors: Black & White
  • Secondary colors: Plum & Dark Sea Green
